What is Kindletter?

It's the Weekly Newsletter full of kindness and support. You'll get dose of motivation right into your inbox!

What is my goal?

The goal of Kindletter is to make you feel less lonely and provide you with the motivation to keep going. If at least one person becomes a little happier, then this goal will have been achieved. The mission is to spread positivity and kindness, even if it's just a small gesture. I want to help people feel connected and supported, especially during challenging times. My hope is that receiving a Kindletter will brighten your day and remind you that you are not alone.
